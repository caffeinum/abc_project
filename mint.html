About

Non-Chimpz are the chimps that aren’t — because they’ve chosen to be something much more. Long chimp-years of training and study have made them the apex specimens of their digital species. Because of that, they’ve earned their right to the fantasy sports world for Non-Chimpz that launches in 2022.

‘NON’ is an acronym, meaning ‘Noble, Outstanding, and Notorious’, capturing the exceptional nature of the Non-Chimpz compared to their typical mammalian brethren. Each is noble in character, outstanding in ability, and notorious in fame within the jungle.

Becoming exceptional starts with the right mindset. That’s why Non-Chimpz Headz will be launched first, available as 17 777 unique digital collectibles on the Ethereum blockchain. These NFTs come with unique skills, and will eventually grant access to Non-Chimpz Bodyzz, which will be minted at a later date.

By putting mind and body together, each Non-Chimp will realize his true and unique competitive potential.

The Specs

As Malchimp Gladwell once said: “it takes 17 777 chimp hours of intensive practice to achieve mastery of complex skills and materials.” That’s exactly how long it took for every Non-Chimp to shape himself into peak form.

Non-Chimpz come fully prepared with varying accessories, magical effects, clothing items, expressions, and over 220 possible traits. That’s more than ordinary chimps can count.

These primates aren’t just brutes either: they have character too. Non-Chimpz come with all different combinations of 11 non-visual traits that will grant them extra benefits in their exclusive sports fantasy world. Though some Non-Chimpz are more rare than others, every one of them has the potential within to become a hero.

Non-Chimpz are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. All unique and 3D. One Non-Chimp costs 0.0555 ETH.
The Non-Chimp Fantasy soon

As the most exceptional of their kind, the Non-Chimpz will be granted exclusive access to a fantasy game of sports contests. The game is scheduled for release in 2022, when the apes will compete for supremacy in various fantasy betting environments. Different combinations of each Non-Chimpz’ visual and non-visual traits will give them different competitive advantages in each contest environment.

More details on the game will be released in early December, after selling the Non-Chimpz Bodyzz.
